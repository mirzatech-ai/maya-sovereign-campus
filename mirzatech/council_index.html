▶ Q&A

Buyer's questions.

Why 12 LLMs and not just one?
A single model echoes its own training. The Council exists to surface BLIND SPOTS · disagreements that single-model chat will never produce. The signal is in the dissent, not the consensus.
What if a model fails or returns garbage?
Every seat has a 3-tier fallback chain. Primary fails → backup #1 fires → backup #2 fires. If all 3 fail (rare), customer sees a [QUORUM FAILURE] flag and gets refunded. Anything less than full quorum is unacceptable.
How is this different from ChatGPT or Claude?
ChatGPT is one model · same family · same biases. The Council is 12 frontier models from 12 different labs answering simultaneously, then synthesized for you. The disagreement is the product.
How long does a round take?
~60 seconds for a typical round. Some thinking models take longer (thinking-class seats can hit 90-120s). All seats run in parallel — total time is bounded by the slowest seat, not the sum.
Can I customize the seat lineup?
Standard: locked 12 seats above. Enterprise: yes — add Claude Opus, GPT-5, custom fine-tuned models, your own provider keys. Talk to Sales for setup.
What's the difference between Council and Parliament?
Council = 12 seats · 1 round · 60 sec · strategic decisions. Parliament = live on mirzatech.ai · 22 minds · 5 rounds · structured debate · for grand questions where dissent needs to play out over multiple turns.
Where does my data go?
Your question + transcript stored in your encrypted account. We don't train on customer data without written consent. EU/US/UK data residency on Enterprise.
Who built this?
Mo Osmanovic (Emaaa LLC). The Council was the methodology Mo used to build his Superio Network · ai-staffing.agency · iamsuperio.com · opencrest.io · osman.is. Now packaged for you.
